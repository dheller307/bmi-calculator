Body Mass Index

Body Mass Index, frequently referred to as BMI, is a calculation used to estimate body fat percentage based on a person's height and weight.

Key

BMI = Weight in Kilograms divided by Height in Meters.

Underweight: less than 18.5, Normal weight: 18.5-24.9, Overweight: 25-29.9, and Obese: 30 or higher.

Important Note

BMI does not take all factors into account and consulting a healthcare proffesional is required for a more comprehensive assessment.
